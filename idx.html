Introduction
Through this website I make publicly available some softwares I've developed, some material I've created for lectures and presentations, my publications and my full CV. There is also a personal section devoted not only to my delight but also to my family and close friends.

I am a first year graduate student at Harvard University - Organismic and Evolutionary Biology department. My advisor is prof. Daniel Hartl.

I was born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil and lived there my entire life. Therefore, I love soccer.
